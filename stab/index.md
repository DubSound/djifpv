# Best GoPro Settings for Cinematic FPV

## Hypersmooth GoPro Hero 7 / 8 Settings

- Resolution: 2.7K (GoPro Hero 7) – 4K (GoPro Hero 8)
- **Aspect Ratio: 4:3**
- **FOV: Wide**
- Frame Rate: 30 – 60
- Shutter (Fixed!):
  - 1/60 or 1/120 for 30p
  - 1/120 or 1/240 for 60p
- **Stabilization: Auto**
- **ProTune: On**
- EV Comp: 0
- White Balance: 4500K – 5500K
- **ISO Min: 100**
- **ISO Max: 100**
- **Sharpness: Low**
- **Color: Flat**
- RAW Audio: Off
- Mics: Wind
